about

I am an experienced Overwatch coach with a demonstrated history of working with teams in the T3 and T2 scene. I pride myself in my ability to teach concepts primarily via various visual mediums, whether that be VOD reviews, info graphics or web documents. For students with differing learning styles, I am able to accomodate them through hands-on drills and open discussions.

I love to learn and teach, I learn more by teaching. I spend hours scouring over vods and researching new strategies. All with the purpose of demonstrating what I absorb back to my students. My ultimate goal is to help players with their fundamental understanding of the game so they can make better decisions in-game, as a team or as an individual.

My personal understanding of the game stems from a more abstract point of view. I try to approach theory from outside the box. I would consider myself a jack of all trades. I am confident in my macro, micro, communications, mental, tactical and strategic coaching equally.
